Welcome to the area of the website which is for information about the TTN (Tactile Times Newspaper) Fantasy Football league.
If you like technology and football you might be interested in Fantasy Football.
The Tactile Times runs a Fantasy Football league.

Getting started

  1. Create a Premier League account, if you don't already have one.
  2. Pick your team of 15 players with your budget of £100 million (not actual money)
  3. Click Leagues and then join a private league. Enter the league code when asked.

You should be a new member of our league!

FAQS

Q: What is Fantasy Football?

A: Fantasy football is a free game you play on the Premier League website or app. You pick a team of 15 Premier League players and if they score a goal, do an assist, etc... You get points.

Q: How do you join?


A: You first need to create an account at the Premier League website or app. You are then given a budget of 100 million pounds protend money to buy 15 players with. After you have saved your team you can then join leagues.

Q: How do I join the Tactile Times league?

A: If it is near the beginning of a season (August to October) the league code will be displayed above these FAQ's and if it is outside of this time, you will be able to register your interest so you do not miss the deadline again.

Q: How do you download the app?


A: You can download the app by searching for "Premier League - Official App" on the Apple App Store or Google Play Store.

Q: Where are the rules?


A: You can find the rules in the help section on the website.
If you have any remaining questions about Fantasy Premier League, please Get in touch or check the rules to see if your question is answered there. If we cannot answer your question, we may ask you to contact the Fantasy Premier League support email address.
