Music

Ronan's composition to go with Issue 4

My name is Ronan. I am 8 years old. I play piano. I love sound because I see with my ears. This piece is called Cacophonous Mind. It sounds like my mind because I hear music everywhere, all the time. I want people to feel excitement and connection to their body when they listen.
I used a tactile mixing board to write down what I hear in my head. I recorded my favorite sounds around the house to make the rhythm track. They are shape sorter, tap, and microwave. I improvised at the piano while listening to the rhythm track. I chose my favorite motifs and I made variations. I added them to my tactile mixing board. I like composing very much, because I express myself.
