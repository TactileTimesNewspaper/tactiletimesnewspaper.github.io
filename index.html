Top Tips for lockdown



  • Learn a piece of music (e.g. somewhere over the rainbow) to play during clap for carers
  • Make things
  • Stay active. It's easy enough to work out at home and it keeps us moving so we don't spend our days in bed.
  • Stay in contact with people as much as possible. Stay in physical contact with the people you're with and stay connected with others online.
  • Keep doing the things that matter to you
  • Play in the garden (if you have one)
  • We're all in this together so talking about how you feel to someone else might help.
  • Learn a new language
  • Lockdown doesn't mean no birthday. You could go quite a walk, organise a group call or bake a cake.
  • Listen to a podcast about something you're interested in. There are loads of podcasts out there on different topics.
    We recommend that you find a podcast about something you're interested in.

Where to start exploring the website?

If you don't know very much about the Tactile Times, why not start on the about us page where you can find out about who we are ore browse our FAQS?
