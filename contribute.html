Contribute an article

If you are a young person who is blind or visually impaired you are welcome to contribute an article, joke or puzzle to the Tactile Times.
You can contribute anything that you think is suitable for the Tactile Times based on past issues. If you think your article is not ready to go in the newspaper yet, edit it.
Copy deadlines and publishing dates are available in the useful information part of the website.

Once you have written your article Contact us and attach your article to the email you send us.
